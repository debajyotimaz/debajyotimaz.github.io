Bio

Debajyoti Mazumder is an NLP researcher with a passion for advancing the understanding of language through computational methods. Currently pursuing a Ph.D. at IISER Bhopal, his work focuses on multilinguality, fine-tuning pretrained models, and in-context learning. With a strong foundation in mathematics and data science, he combines theoretical rigor with practical applications to tackle complex problems in NLP.
